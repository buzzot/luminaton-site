Our Products

Lighting solutions, tailored to your project

Choose from our standard inventory or work with our engineering team to build a fully customized LED solution — color temperature, voltage, IP rating, and module shape all to spec.

Customized LED Modules

High-output SMD modules engineered for channel letters, light boxes, and architectural signage. Available in 2835, 3030, 5050 chips and custom PCB layouts.

  • 12V / 24V DC options
  • IP65 / IP67 waterproof rated
  • 2700K – 6500K + RGB / RGBW
  • Custom string length & spacing

LED Strip Lights

Flexible strip lighting for cove, undercabinet, accent, and decorative use. From economy reels to high-CRI Pro Series with cuttable, solderable, dimmable options.

  • SMD 2835, 3528, 5050, 5630
  • 60 / 120 / 240 LEDs per meter
  • CRI 90+ available
  • Cuttable every 25 – 100 mm

Power Supplies & Accessories

Drivers, dimmers, controllers, connectors, and aluminum profiles to complete every installation. Matched to our modules and strips for guaranteed compatibility.

  • Constant voltage drivers 30W – 400W
  • RF / Wi-Fi / DMX controllers
  • Aluminum extrusions & diffusers
  • Waterproof connectors
